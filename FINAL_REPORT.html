1. What the game is about:

At present, the gameplay is not terribly exciting. What you can do is visit various planets and star systems generated by the system. The intended economic activity was highly dependent on this component, so it was constructed first.

Nonetheless, the generation is somewhat interesting, particularly in that we generate some text and graphics as well as astronomical data for a condensed (not particularly realistic) galaxy.

2. What is procedurally generated:

Please refer to the README for a fuller listing.

We have procedurally generated stars, planets, names for all stars and planets, and noise-based textures for them.

3. Other techniques implemented:

We have a form of motion planning implemented that steers around planets. Our pilot is not particularly willing to leave the solar system's plane, so he never takes the easy way.

4. What are the limitations and issues with the game:

At present, minimap selection doesn't take you to the right place, the highlight on the minimap is also not working correctly, and once you travel to another star system, interplanetary travel doesn't seem to work properly. Loading a star system is regrettably slow due to the texture generation--this could be alleviated rather well by using worker threads.
